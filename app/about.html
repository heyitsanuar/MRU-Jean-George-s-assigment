OUR HISTORY

Jean Georges was an attempt to break into the resturaunt market by industry professionals who were unable to find a resturaunt which could suit the needs of the many people of New York. In 2015 the resturaunt was founded and since then Jean Georges has recieved critical acclaim as well as popularity with the busy people of New York.

OUR QUALITY

With hundreds of positive reviews and numerous awards given to us, Jean Georges has a proven reputation for both good service, and good food at any time of day.
